Brighton Mainline modernisation

Over Christmas and New Year, we continued modernising the railway and signalling equipment on the Brighton Mainline.

Together with re-building a bridge at Old Lodge Lane in Purley, this work is all part of our Railway Upgrade Plan to bring passengers more reliable, comfortable and quieter journeys.
